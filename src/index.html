Overview: An Intel Celeron E7500 clocked at 2.93 GHz with 8 GB of DDR3 RAM. 250 GB of SSD storage, sitting on a MoBo with 1 GB/s onboard ethernet.

Description: This small PC fits snugly into practically any space you choose. With an Intel Celeron E3300, this PC won't produce much heat, nor be too noisy for when you're browsing the internet and doing light tasks. With 8 GB of DDR3 RAM, you will be able to access any web page or file you could load, and with 250 GB of storage, you'll have enough space to store any important files.
